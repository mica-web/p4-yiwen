Type of donation

Donate Items

Second Chance can give new life to old doors and windows, hardware and tools, ironwork and mantles, and gently used furniture. If you have building materials, kitchen appliances, bathroom fixtures, tubs, sinks, accessories, household goods, garden elements, collections, lighting or unique architectural items in good to excellent condition, please consider donating them to Second Chance. Working vehicles, items with historical value, and certain “oddities” are welcome, too.

We enjoy building ongoing donor relationships with various businesses and organizations, and we’re proud to have so many repeat donors who value the efficiency and excellence we’ve gained through two decades of experience. Not only do donors benefit from a tax deduction, but they contribute to the growth of an organization that is changing lives through job training, salvaging our regional architectural history, saving consumers money, and protecting the planet through resource conservation and waste stream diversion.

Donate a House

If you’d like a no-pressure consultation to learn more about our deconstruction services or the significant tax and other benefits associated with deconstructing rather demolishing a home or building, please fill in the requested information below. A trained Second Chance team member will contact you to learn about your project and help you understand the process and rewards of donating a structure to us to use in our job training program. You may also call us at 410-385-1700 to discover if home donation is right for you.

Donate Money

Because Second Chance, Inc. is an IRS recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, all monetary gifts shared with us are tax deductible. They are also greatly appreciated, because it takes substantial revenue to operate and expand our social enterprise. To help us facilitate more “second chances” through a financial contribution of any amount, please use our PayPal "Donate" Link or fill out the following form and a SC representative will contact you to facilitate your gift.

Donate Time

We realize our mission is too broad to accomplish by ourselves. Your talents, passions, and availability can have a profound impact on individuals and the local community through Second Chance. Your specialized experience can create opportunities for more and better second chances, while simply showing up with a smile and willingness to work is fantastic, too. We have plenty of “general help” needs to keep you busy. Groups from 2 to 200 can be plugged into the action, and they consistently report a positive and rewarding service experience.

To volunteer or find out more about it, please fill out the form below. Volunteers and groups are here every week, and we look forward to integrating you into the Second Chance family, too!
